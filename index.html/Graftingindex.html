Steps

  1. Select the right rootstock and scion for compatibility.
  2. Choose a suitable grafting method (e.g., cleft, whip and tongue, or bark graft).
  3. Make precise cuts on the rootstock and scion to ensure a snug fit.
  4. Secure the graft union tightly to promote healing and integration.
  5. Protect the graft from sun exposure and pests with grafting wax or tape.
  6. Monitor the graft regularly for signs of growth and adjust care as needed.

Material

  • Wax
  • Rubber bud strips
  • waxcoated cotton twine
  • Grafting tape

Tools

  • Grafting knife
  • Secateures
